Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information DoReDog (“we”, “us”, “the site” — reachable at doredog.com) collects when you use it, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We’ve tried to write it in plain language. If anything is unclear, email us at mnkahraman@gmail.com and we’ll be glad to explain.

1. The short version

  • You can browse and play everything on DoReDog without creating an account.
  • We don’t ask for your name, address, or payment details — we don’t sell anything.
  • Like most free sites, we use advertising and basic analytics, which rely on cookies and similar technologies.
  • We never sell your personal information.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

The only time you actively send us information is when you choose to email us (for example, via the contact page). In that case we receive your email address and whatever you write, and we use it only to reply to you.

Information collected automatically

When you visit, our hosting provider and analytics tools may automatically record standard technical data such as your browser type, device and operating system, approximate region, the pages you view, and the date and time of your visit. This helps us understand what’s popular and keep the site working.

3. Cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small files stored by your browser. We (and our partners) use them to remember basic preferences, measure traffic, and — where enabled — serve and personalise advertising. You can block or delete cookies at any time in your browser settings; the site will still work, though some things (like remembering settings) may not.

4. Advertising

DoReDog is supported by advertising. We may work with third-party advertising partners (for example, Google AdSense) to display ads. These partners may use cookies, device identifiers and similar technologies to show ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits. You can review and opt out of personalised Google advertising at adssettings.google.com.
  • For opt-out options across many ad networks, see aboutads.info/choices or youronlinechoices.eu.

5. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to see aggregate trends — for example, which pieces get played most. This data is used in aggregate and is not used to personally identify you.

6. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain and improve DoReDog.
  • To understand which content is useful so we can add more of it.
  • To display advertising that keeps the site free.
  • To respond to messages you send us.

7. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers that make the site run (such as our host, analytics and advertising partners), and only as needed for those services — or where the law requires it.

8. Children

DoReDog is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we’ll remove it.

9. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct or delete personal information we hold about you, or to object to certain processing. Because we don’t maintain accounts, the main lever is your browser’s cookie and privacy controls. For any request, email mnkahraman@gmail.com.

10. Data retention & security

We keep automatically-collected analytics only as long as it’s useful, and emails only as long as needed to help you. We take reasonable measures to protect information, though no method of transmission over the internet is ever completely secure.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves. When we do, we’ll change the “last updated” date above. Significant changes will be highlighted on this page.

12. Contact

Questions about privacy? Write to mnkahraman@gmail.com. We read every message.
